I am a PhD student at the Robotic Interactive Perception Lab with Guillermo Gallego, affiliated with Technical University Berlin and the research center SCIoI. Previously, I did my Master in Electrical Engineering at RWTH Aachen University.

News

  • Sep 2023: I am doing a 6-months research stay at the UPenn GRASP lab with professor Kostas Daniilidis.
  • Jun 2023: I presented our work on animal behavior quantification with event cameras at the CVPR 2023 Workshop on Event-based Vision.
