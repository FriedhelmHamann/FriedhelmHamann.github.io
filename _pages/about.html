I am a PhD student at the Robotic Interactive Perception Lab with Guillermo Gallego, affiliated with Technical University Berlin and the research center SCIoI. I also spent part of my PhD at UPenn GRASP lab with professor Kostas Daniilidis. Previously, I did my Master's in Electrical Engineering at RWTH Aachen University.
